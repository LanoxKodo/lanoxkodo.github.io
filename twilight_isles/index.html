Twilight Isles

Twilight Isles was created and will be actively maintained as a central place for people to to get together and hang out online. Primarily it was formed from the desire to bring people together for the cross-platform Minecraft server I host but it was deemed best to not limit it to just being a private Minecraft Discord server. Everyone is welcome to join, we hope you do and can share in camaraderie we have there!

We plan to host activities, gather users for friendly online events, grow the community, and much more to make the experience pleasant for all parties involved.

This server will always be available to people who are coming with an honest and faithful spirit whom wish to share nothing but wonderful times!
