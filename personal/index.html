Motivations

People know me as a tech junkie of a sort, I love technology in every aspect! People know I identify more as a furry than anything else to which I'm thankful for those that don't scrutinize me for. People know I have a fondness for gaming and modding, especially if I can share those moments with others.
But sometimes not all is ever known, as such I always say that I am an open book for any question you might have.

But if there is one thing I don't think I've ever made clear, everything I do isn't just due to a spark of wanting to show things off. Nay, rather it's all out of the intent to make everyone excited and thrilled with whatever I can show around. I want to create memories with people that they will cherish and will take forward in life with optimism to try something new. I dabble around with a lot of stuff when I'm not gaming, often these projects are talked about because I want others to know even if you are known as someone with a single interest, you can have a mind that runs wild, and free. Creating memories are what I look for so when we all look back we can have a good historical point to smile and crack a joke about. I admire all the friends I can have around and I hope to see that others around me too feel like they have friends they can be happy with.

To all who have been with me through my ups and downs: thank you

I cannot express how thankful I am to have the friends around me at this time and for those I will one day meet. With each passing moment I am thrilled to move forward into tomorrow to work on exciting endeavors and have laughs with people out elsewhere on our home planet.

I sincerely thank everyone whom continues to be a part of my life, each one of you continue to contribute the best of yourselves in being a part of my life to which I in turn can share what I can do make everyone smile. You all are the most kind I could ask for and I hope we all can walk looking forward into the future with smiles as we move about out there!

If you've been here for some time to join me in our adventures, I thank you very much for being a friend for the time we have together. And with that I shall see you when dawn breaks for when we can hang out in a game or vc or whatever else we choose! :)

Sincerely from your fox friend,
